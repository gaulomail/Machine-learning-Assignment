### Typical Interaction Pattern:

1. **Requirement Analysis**
   - Human: Provided assignment requirements and objectives
   - AI: Analyzed requirements and proposed implementation plan

2. **Implementation**
   - Human: Reviewed and approved implementation plan

   - Human: Requested features (e.g., "add premium UI", "optimize training speed")
